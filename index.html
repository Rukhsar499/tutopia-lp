Frequently asked questions

T-SAT (Tutopia Scholastic Assessment Test) is a merit-based scholarship exam for WBBSE students (Class 7–12). It helps students earn scholarships based on their academic performance—not luck or discounts.

Any student studying under WBBSE, from Class 7 to Class 12, can apply—regardless of school or current academic level.

You can get up to 100% scholarship if:
  • You are Rank 1 in your school (based on previous academic results)
  • You submit a verified marksheet
  • You qualify within the limited seats allocated per school

You can still earn a scholarship through the T-SAT online test, based on your performance. Scholarships range from 10% to 60%.

T-SAT is purely merit-based:
  • No random discounts
  • No coupon codes
  • No negotiation
Your scholarship depends only on your performance or academic rank.

The test is designed to assess:
  • Logical reasoning
  • Math aptitude
  • Problem-solving ability
  • Creativity
It’s not about memorization—it’s about how you think.
